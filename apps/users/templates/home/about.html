    About Us

    Welcome to Fantasy Premier League!

    How it Works
    Our Fantasy Premier League allows you to experience the thrill of managing your own football team. Select five players wisely, and watch as they accumulate points based on their real-life performances during Premier League matches.

    Scoring System
    Players earn points for various actions on the field:

    • Match Played: +1 point
    • Goal: +3 points
    • Assist: +2 points
    • Yellow Card: -1 point
    • Red Card: -3 points

    The Quest for Glory
    The ultimate goal is to assemble a team that earns the highest total points. Will your strategic choices lead your team to victory? Keep an eye on the performance of your selected players and make strategic transfers to stay ahead of the competition.

    How to Win
    At the end of each matchday, the team with the highest cumulative points takes the crown. Compete with friends, colleagues, or football enthusiasts from around the world to see who has the best managerial skills.

    Get Started
    Create your team, make smart decisions, and enjoy the excitement of our Fantasy Premier League!
